The Designer

A note from the studio.

LTL STR grew from a small studio table and a refusal to rush. What started as one bag — made for a friend, from leftover leather — became a quiet practice in making things that last. There is no factory line here, no seasonal churn. Just a commitment to honest materials, careful construction, and the belief that accessories should age alongside the people who carry them.

— The LTL STR Studio
